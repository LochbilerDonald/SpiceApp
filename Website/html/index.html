Description

Spice is here to help you find your next dining experience! You can create a user profile to customize the results based on your preferences. Users can also invite each other to events where we handle finding the best experience based on a groups overall prefences. Users could also find a restaurant without preferences, Spice it up, to get something out of the ordinary to break up the monotony.
